───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                        ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• B.E. First Year: 10+2 with Physics, Math and one of Chemistry/Biology/Vocational. 45% for General, 40% for Reserved. Valid non-zero MHT-CET or JEE Main score.
• B.E. Direct Second Year: Polytechnic Diploma or B.Sc. with 45% (General) or 40% (Reserved).
• M.E. First Year: Relevant B.E./B.Tech degree with 45% (General) or 40% (Reserved). GATE score mandatory for CAP seats.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                  INFRASTRUCTURE & FACILITIES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Campus: 10-12 acres of modern infrastructure.
• Academic Infrastructure: 16 digital classrooms/seminar halls, 180 academic computers. Integration with Google Workspace and Google Classroom.
• Central Library: 15,000+ books, research reports, e-library, computerized access, and a large reading hall.
• Residential: Separate boys and girls hostels (100 students capacity, double/triple sharing).
• Other Amenities: Clean canteen, Auditorium, Transport facility for nearby areas.
• Spiritual Centers: Bhagwan Mahaveer Temple and a Meditation Centre for holistic well-being.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                  DEPARTMENT LABORATORIES

• Civil Labs: Engineering Mechanics, Strength of Materials, Fluid Mechanics, Transportation, Concrete Technology, Geotechnical, Environmental, CAD, Geology, Surveying.

• Electrical Labs: Electrical Machines, Power Systems, Control Systems, Circuit Analysis, Power Electronics, Microprocessor.
• Mechanical Labs/Workshop: Dynamics of Machine, Heat Transfer, Energy Conversion (IC engines), Refrigeration & AC, Material Science, Measurement System, Central Workshop (foundry, welding).
