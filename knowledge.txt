───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                    ENGINEERING PROGRAMS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Sanmati Engineering College offers the following B.E. programs
(4-year duration, VTU affiliated):

1. Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) — 120 seats
2. Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) — 60 seats
3. Mechanical Engineering (ME) — 60 seats
4. Civil Engineering (CE) — 60 seats
5. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML) — 60 seats

ENGINEERING FEE STRUCTURE (per year):
• Tuition Fee: ₹55,000 — ₹75,000 (varies by branch)
• CSE & AI&ML: ₹75,000 per year
• ECE: ₹65,000 per year
• ME & CE: ₹55,000 per year
• Development Fee: ₹5,000 per year
• Exam Fee: ₹3,000 per year (paid to VTU)

ELIGIBILITY:
• Passed 12th (PCM) with minimum 45% aggregate (40% for reserved)
• Valid CET / COMEDK / KCET / JEE Main score
• Admission through KEA counselling or management quota

SCHOLARSHIPS:
• Government scholarships for SC/ST/OBC students (full fee waiver)
• Sanmati Merit Scholarship: Top 10 CET/COMEDK scorers get 50% tuition waiver
• Sports quota: 25% fee concession for state-level athletes
